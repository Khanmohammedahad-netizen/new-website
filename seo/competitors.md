# Competitor Benchmark Notes

Benchmark set: Toptal, Vention, BairesDev, Thoughtbot, DockYard, Netguru, 10Pearls, Andela, Intellectsoft, ScienceSoft, Brainhub.

## What they all do that we now do
- Unique title/description per page ✅
- Organization schema ✅
- Case studies with narrative structure ✅
- Clear CTAs ✅

## What they do that we don't yet (gap list)
1. **Server-rendered HTML** — every one of them; we're client-only SPA (Phase 2 priority)
2. **Services pages per offering** — deep, individually-ranking pages (Phase 3)
3. **Industry pages** (ScienceSoft dominates here) (Phase 3)
4. **Blog/insights engine** — Netguru/Brainhub rank via content clusters (Phase 4)
5. **Named team/authors + about page** — E-E-A-T anchor (Phase 3)
6. **Client logos + named testimonials** (Phase 3, needs client permission)
7. **Pricing/engagement-model page** — Toptal converts with this (consider)

## Our edges to press
- Genuinely shipped AI-agent products (MAK OS, LeadMine) — most agencies only write ABOUT agents; we can show receipts
- Speed: 6-week ERP delivery story
- India+UAE+UK corridor positioning — less saturated than pure-US targeting
